# Partner Targets for VaultCal

## Partner Integration Roadmap

| Partner/API                | Free-Tier Limits                       | Integration Effort | Value-Add (User Job Solved)                                   | Rationale                                                                                      |
|----------------------------|---------------------------------------|--------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Nextcloud**              | 2 users, 2GB storage                  | M                  | Secure file storage and sharing for contacts and calendars    | Nextcloud offers a robust self-hosted solution that complements VaultCal's security focus.   |
| **Radicale**               | Open-source, no limits                | S                  | Calendar and contact management                                | Direct integration with Radicale enhances the core functionality of VaultCal.                 |
| **Zapier**                 | 5 Zaps, 100 tasks                     | M                  | Automation of tasks between VaultCal and other apps          | Zapier allows users to connect VaultCal with numerous other services, increasing usability.   |
| **Google Calendar API**    | 1 million requests/month              | L                  | Syncing events and contacts with Google services              | Provides a familiar interface for users transitioning from Google services to VaultCal.       |
| **Stripe**                 | Free to start, transaction fees apply | M                  | Payment processing for premium features                       | Integrating Stripe allows for monetization of premium features, enhancing revenue potential.   |
| **Slack API**              | 10 integrations                       | S                  | Notifications and reminders for calendar events               | Enhances user engagement by providing real-time updates and reminders via Slack.              |
| **Trello API**             | 10 boards, 250 cards                  | M                  | Task management integration for calendar events               | Allows users to manage tasks related to calendar events, improving productivity.               |
| **Mailgun**                | 5,000 emails/month for 3 months       | M                  | Email notifications for calendar events                       | Enables users to receive email reminders and notifications, enhancing the overall experience.  |

### Prioritization Rationale
1. **Nextcloud** and **Radicale** are prioritized due to their alignment with the self-hosting and security focus of VaultCal.
2. **Zapier** and **Google Calendar API** provide significant value by enhancing usability and integration with widely used services.
3. **Stripe** is included for its potential to generate revenue through premium features, which is crucial for sustainability.
4. **Slack** and **Trello** integrations enhance user engagement and productivity, making VaultCal more appealing to potential users.
5. **Mailgun** adds value by improving communication and reminders, which are essential for calendar management.

This roadmap aims to create a robust ecosystem around VaultCal, enhancing its functionality while addressing user needs effectively.
