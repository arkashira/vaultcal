# Business Model Canvas for VaultCal

## Customer Segments
- Individuals and small businesses seeking secure self-hosted contact and calendar solutions.
- Tech-savvy users interested in privacy-focused applications.
- Organizations in sectors with strict data protection regulations (e.g., healthcare, finance).

## Value Propositions
- Security-focused: Provides built-in guidance and protection features for self-hosting contacts and calendars.
- Self-hosted solution: Users maintain control over their data without relying on third-party services.
- User-friendly setup: Simplifies the installation and management of Radicale for non-technical users.

## Channels
- Direct sales through the official website and GitHub repository.
- Online communities and forums focused on privacy and self-hosting (e.g., Reddit, specialized tech forums).
- Partnerships with privacy-focused blogs and influencers for reviews and promotions.

## Customer Relationships
- Community support through forums and GitHub issues for troubleshooting and feature requests.
- Regular updates and newsletters to inform users about new features and security practices.
- Premium support options for businesses requiring dedicated assistance.

## Revenue Streams
- One-time purchase fee for the software license (estimated $50 - $100 USD).
- Subscription model for premium support and updates (estimated $5 - $15 USD/month).
- Potential future revenue from add-on features or integrations.

## Key Resources
- Development team skilled in security and self-hosting technologies.
- GitHub repository for version control and community contributions.
- Marketing resources for outreach and community engagement.

## Key Activities
- Continuous development and improvement of the VaultCal software.
- Marketing efforts to raise awareness and attract users.
- Community engagement to gather feedback and foster user loyalty.

## Key Partners
- Open-source communities and contributors to enhance software features.
- Privacy-focused organizations for collaboration and promotion.
- Hosting providers offering services compatible with VaultCal.

## Cost Structure
- Development costs (salaries, tools, and infrastructure) estimated at $100,000 USD annually.
- Marketing expenses (advertising, partnerships, and community outreach) estimated at $20,000 USD annually.
- Support and maintenance costs (customer support, updates) estimated at $15,000 USD annually.
