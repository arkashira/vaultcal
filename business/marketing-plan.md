# Marketing Plan for VaultCal

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
VaultCal is a security-focused, self-hosted contact and calendar server setup and management tool that empowers users with built-in guidance and protection features.

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**  
1. VaultCal: Your secure solution for self-hosting contacts and calendars with expert guidance.
2. Take control of your data with VaultCal, the self-hosted management tool for contacts and calendars.
3. VaultCal: Securely manage your contacts and calendars with confidence and ease.
